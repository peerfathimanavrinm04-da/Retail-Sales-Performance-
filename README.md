#### The Retail Sales Performance Dashboard is designed to provide a clear, interactive view of retail business performance. It consolidates sales data into meaningful insights, enabling stakeholders to quickly identify revenue drivers, monitor trends, and evaluate product and regional performance.

### Summary of Dashboard Components

### 1. Key Metrics (Summary Cards)

- **Total Sales:** Overall revenue generated.
- **Total Quantity Sold:** Total units sold across all products.
- **Average Sales per Order:** Efficiency indicator showing average revenue per transaction.

### 2. Visualizations

- **Sales by Region (Bar Chart):** Compares revenue across North, South, East, and West regions.
- **Sales by Product Category (Pie Chart):** Shows category contribution (Electronics, Furniture, Clothing).
- **Sales by Day (Line Chart):** Tracks daily sales trends, highlighting peaks and dips.
- **Top Products by Sales (Table/List):** Displays the highest‑performing products, e.g., Laptop, Smartphone, Shirt.

### 3. Filters (Slicers)

- **Customer Type:** New vs. Returning customers.
- **Product Category:** Drill down into specific categories.
- **Region:** Focus on regional performance.
